USPS Template Tool

The USPS Template Tool was created to assist consultants with note taking and guidance while on a call. The tool allows consultants to follow a certain script while interacting with business owners. Consultants can ask the correct probing questions to determine what USPS service is right for them. The tool was tested on 15 employees and the use of the tool was positive. The client was happy that the notes were unified and they were easy to read. The template tool has had several additions and modifications – most recently having internal links to email templates, consultant extension directory, and external resources.

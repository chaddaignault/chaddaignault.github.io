CashStar

CashStar became a client of Sutherland's in July 2012. The website was first created to cascade important information to employees about recent events, brand promotions, contact information for brands, and other useful resources to provide employees a quick way to find and deliver information to customers. This allowed employees to input information, including order number, brand, reason for call, and outcome, and allowed management to export these details and provide to CashStar. The site contains static pages, dynamic news content, form submissions, and an administration section for maintenance and data exports.

What Was Done/Used

HTML5 • CSS3 • Bootstrap (certain portions/redesign) • PHP • MySQL

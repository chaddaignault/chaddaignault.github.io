Doan's Honey Farm

The Doan's Honey Farm website was a project I was asked to do in early 2012. A member of the Doan family asked me to create a website that provided content the family provided and was able to be found by search engines. The website was going to expand and have a blog and online store, however a situation happened within the family and the website was ultimately scrapped and taken offline in the summer of 2012.

What Was Done/Used

  • Front-End Development
  • Back-End Development
  • Visual Design
  • PHP Programming

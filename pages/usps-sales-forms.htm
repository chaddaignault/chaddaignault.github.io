USPS Sales Forms

The USPS Inside Sales account at Sutherland was spread across three locations in the United States. With three differen locations housing less than 100 each, a Sales Verification team was created to review and check employee sales. This online form allowed the Sales Verification team to enter sales into a database where it was stored and reviewed by management to calculate commissions. This was also used by the Quality team for training and coaching purposes.

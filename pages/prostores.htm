ProStores

ProStores became a client of Sutherland's in April 2009, when they began to migrate their SaaS eCommerce platform to a new data center. From the beginning I implemented an internal website to cascade important information to employees. Content found on the website enabled the employee to quickly find and deliver information to a customer. The website consists of static pages, dynamic news content, form submissions and an administration section for maintenance and data exports.

I left the ProStores account in December 2010 when I was promoted to Manager on a different account. In February 2015, ProStores officially shut down.

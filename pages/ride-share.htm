Ride Share

The ride share board was a project that first started at MVCC. The administration wanted a way for students to be able to carpool with each other when gas prices were rising in 2005-2006. The Marketing & Communications team create the ride share/carpool site where students could offer rides and view available rides.

A few years later a Team Manager at Sutherland Global had the same idea. The idea was created, and the ride share website was created. Due to company policy, the ride share board was unable to be launched due to Human Resources being afraid of employees blaming the company for any accidents that may occur. The ride share board was never launched, however I was able to expand my abilities in PHP and MySQL. The board was redone in 2014 using Bootstrap.

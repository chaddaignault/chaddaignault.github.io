Do It For Dougie

Fighting cancer is a team effort, and thats what the DoItForDougie.org website is all about. The “MV Power Table” crew came together to raise money for the Relay for Life. T-shirts, banners, and a web presence were created for this event. Supporting the team, we became apart of a life-changing event that celebrates the lives who have battled cancer. We all will remember our friend, Jeremy in our own way. This site, is a dedication to him and will have information on the things that we’ll be doing to keep his spirit alive.
